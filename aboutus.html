Who are we?

A young, new, dynamic and professional reality. A friendly environment, where everyone, all ages, shapes and colors, can train in peace and serenity. Fitness Club aims to guarantee everyone the possibility of exercising safely and with the certainty of results, whatever they are. A team composed of a nutritionist to support you in your diet and a physiotherapist, personal trainer, for any need. Thanks to the professionalism of its gym instructors, and the transparency of the offers, it is aimed at all those who are tired of not achieving results, of not being sufficiently followed in the gym, of having to pay supplements for each service or, more simply, for those who really wanted something new! We think that preparation is an important aspect of this work. Our customers entrust us with their hopes, make us participants in their expectations, they rely on us to achieve their goals, whether they lose a few pounds, improve their physical appearance or simply feel better and fitter. We have a nutritionist, a physiotherapist and the staff of the various rooms where the actual physical activity takes place.
